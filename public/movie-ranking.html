The Best Movies According to Suheera

My top 3 movies of all-time.

1.SNOW WHITE AND THE 7 DWARF

A beautiful but orphaned princess, Snow White, lives with her stepmother, the wicked Queen, who previously relegated her to servitude

2.CINDRELLA

The protagonist is a young woman living in forsaken circumstances that are suddenly changed to remarkable fortune, with her ascension to the throne via marriage.

3.BEAUTY AND THE BEAST

The plot of Beauty and The Beast is about a handsome prince that has a spell cast over him, and that spell turns him into a beast.
